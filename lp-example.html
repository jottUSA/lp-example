Brand Ambassador
 

Are you an active member of the uTest Community seeking an opportunity to step up your regional involvement as a Brand Ambassador? This may just be the perfect role if you’re an enthusiastic, outgoing uTester who enjoys engaging others in the testing community, have a strong social media presence and have a passion to represent the uTest brand!

  • Role: uTest Brand Ambassador
  • Duration: Ongoing, Part Time
  • Location: Remote but Mexico, India, China, Japan, France, or Spain.


Qualifications


  • Written and spoken English fluency; proficient in at least one local dialect
  • Engaging, supportive and outgoing personality
  • 1 year active testing experience on uTest.com
  • Access to social media or online forums relevant to QA testing
  • Hands-on experience with email and other online communication tools such as Slack, Skype, Hangouts, etc.
  • Strong knowledge and understanding of cultural norms in your local market


Preferred Skills & Experience:


  • Experience in event planning a huge plus!
  • Strong background or experience in marketing, communications or relevant field
  • Experience and/or willingness to partner with local agencies to boost uTest awareness and presence
  • Previous work experience as a brand ambassador, promoter or outreach manager


Duties and Responsibilities:


  • Represent uTest in your local community:
    • Host local events and meetups
    • Work with local organizations and institutions to grow the community
    • Act as a liaison to the Community Management team by providing insights into the local and adjacent markets
  • Recruit and onboard testers from your local community:
    • Track and encourage new community members in your country through the onboarding and training process
    • Aid in the engagement and training of the new members you bring to the community
    • Manage email and tester onboarding for incoming testers in your country
    • Provide TTL support in cycles where your recruits are engaged
    • Take charge on basic local device management
  • Nurture the already-existing uTest community in your country:
    • Act as a point of contact for current testers and new testers joining the uTest community


Benefits Include:


  • Swag
  • Budget to host local events
  • TTL Work
  • Badge on Profile
  • Compensation for time
  • Additional bonuses for new users recruited into the community
  • Direct communication with Community Management team
